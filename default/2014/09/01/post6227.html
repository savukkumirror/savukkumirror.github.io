We are back

Sep 1, 2014

So we are back now, friends

Clearly judicial proclamations are not going to hold us back.
We stress again the dispute was over savukku.net, even where we have challenged the ruling. A PIL filed on the issue is also pending. Nothing that can be considered offensive has been carried by savukku DOT in.  We have only stored previously published articles for archival purposes. In the circumstances, we consider it vindictive to ban the latter too.
In any case thanks to modern technology, you can’t close down any site that easily. So we will keep springing up  from the unlikliest of corners to carry on with our mission – that of demanding justice and unmasking vested interests.
Hope readers will continue to extend their co-operation.
Thanks again everyone of those helping out.
We shall overcome
SAVUKKU EDITORIAL TEAM
